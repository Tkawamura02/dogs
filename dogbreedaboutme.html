Author Information

My name is Tiffany Kawamura (she/her) and I am an Arizona State University Junior who was born and raised in Hawaii. My major is Biomedical Sciences and my minor is Applied Computing. My current job is through Arizona State University as a call representative for Blue Cross Blue Shield of Arizona, a major health insurance company for Arizona. My hobbies entail taking care of my six dogs, learning languages, studying, reading, and travelling.

Why did you create this website?

I have spent my entire life studying dogs and their behavior. I know a lot about how to train them, take care of them, and communicate with them with body language. I have spent my whole life around them. You can say that I am able to communicate with dogs than humans because of this. It was family history to always have dogs so I kind of took it as it was and continued my dog owner legacy.

What did you want to be when you were younger?

You know this question is hard to answer. I wanted to be everything when I was younger at one point. But I guess, when I was younger, I believed that I would be a surgeon.

How do you like to relax?

I like to relax by reading, sleeping, hanging out with my dogs, cooking, and studying. Anything which requires brain activity to be focused on one sole purpose is what I consider to be relaxing since my brain is a pretty noisy environment to be in!

Who are you most inspired by?

I am most inspired by my dad. Honestly, it was not always this way because I used to wholeheartedly hate him when I was younger. But when I grew up, I worked on my relationship with him and noticed that a lot of his personalities and tendencies were passed down to me. I was able to relate to him on a different level than my mom.
